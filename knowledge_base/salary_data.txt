FACTORS THAT SIGNIFICANTLY AFFECT SALARY
------------------------------------------

Upward factors (increase salary):
  + PhD from a top university: +15-30% for research roles
  + Specialisation in high-demand areas (LLMs, GenAI, MLOps)
  + Big tech or well-known startup experience on CV
  + Proficiency in multiple cloud platforms
  + 2+ years of production ML experience
  + Spanish, German, or French language skills in respective countries

Downward factors (may reduce offers):
  - Bootcamp as primary credential without strong portfolio
  - Long gaps in employment without explanation
  - Narrow skill set (only one domain or technology)
  - Inability to communicate technical findings clearly
